About Airambition
World's No.1 Air Hostess Training Institute.
The World's No.1 and most prestigious Air Hostess Training Institute. Established in 1990 as a premium Air Hostess Training Institute, Airambition today is recognized as a pioneer and innovator in air hostess training all over India and abroad. The company offers a wide range of Courses to aspiring students and also provides them with exceptional placement support. Airambition has cemented its place as an elite training company in the fields of Aviation Hospitality, Travel Management, and Customer Service.
